About me

I am an Argentine physicist working at the intersection of quantum mechanics, chaos theory, and complex systems. My research explores how classical chaos manifests in quantum systems and how information propagates through complex networks.


I hold a PhD in Physical Sciences from the Universidad de Buenos Aires (UBA) and have conducted postdoctoral research and multiple guest researcher stays at the Laboratoire de Physique Théorique (Université Paul Sabatier) in Toulouse, France, in collaboration with Prof. Dima Shepelyansky.

  • PhD Universidad de Buenos Aires
  • Postdoc Univ. Paul Sabatier, Toulouse
  • Award Stimulus Prize – ANCEFN (2019)
  • Papers 55+ in international journals
  • Role Reviewer for scientific journals
